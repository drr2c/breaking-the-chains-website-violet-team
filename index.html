Breaking The Chains: Human Trafficking in Brazil

Welcome to Breaking The Chains

Welcome to Breaking The Chains, an educational site dedicated to raising awareness about human trafficking in Brazil. Here you will find clear information about how trafficking happens, key statistics, and how Brazilian laws and organizations are working to protect vulnerable communities. Our Stories and Data pages bring the issue to life through authentic experiences and visual trends. At the same time, the Get Involved section shows you how to recognize the signs, share reliable information, and connect with five trusted anti-trafficking organizations. As this is a student project, we do not collect donations directly. However, you can choose to support these organizations through their official websites.

Support Trusted Organizations

Because this is a student project, we do not accept donations directly. Instead, we encourage you to learn more and, if you are able, support trusted organizations that combat human trafficking and support survivors.

The Exodus Road

An international nonprofit that partners with local communities and authorities to identify trafficking, support rescues, and provide survivor care.

Project Rescue

A global network focused on prevention, safe homes, and long-term healing for individuals and families affected by sexual exploitation and trafficking.
