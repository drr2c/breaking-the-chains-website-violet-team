Get Involved

Everyone Has a Role to Play

Everyone has a role to play in ending human trafficking. Learn the signs, spread awareness, and support organizations working to protect victims and prevent exploitation. Small actions create big change—get involved today.

Support Survivors

Supporting survivors of human trafficking is one of the most meaningful ways to make a difference. While it can sometimes be challenging to find a non-profit organization operating in Brazil, there are anti-trafficking programs that support communities in the region. Contributing to these programs through donations or volunteer work can help provide long-term care that survivors rely on as they try to get back on their feet.

Advocate for Change

Advocating for change is just as important. Individuals can participate in International Awareness Day Against Human Trafficking on January 11th, which is a day dedicated to empowering survivors. Advocates can also call for stronger protection policies and ensure the voices of those affected by trafficking are heard.

Support Trusted Organizations

Even if you are far from Brazil, you can still support anti-trafficking work in the region. Learn more about the organizations below and, when possible, donate or volunteer through their official websites to help strengthen prevention, rescue, and long-term care for survivors.
